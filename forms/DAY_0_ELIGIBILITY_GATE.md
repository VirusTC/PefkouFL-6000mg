# CLINICAL MECHANISM ENTRY MATRIX: DAY 0 ELIGIBILITY GATE
**Protocol ID:** PINE-CVI-01-EA  
**Investigational Product:** PefkouFL-6000mg Standardized *Pinus pinaster* Core Matrix

## 1. MANDATORY INCLUSION GATEWAYS
A subject must meet 100% of the following physiological criteria to qualify for investigational product allocation under the IRB Expanded Access mechanism.

*   **[ ] Diagnostic Confirmation of Vascular Stagnation:** Subject presents with clear objective markers of Chronic Venous Insufficiency (CVI) or Microangiopathy (Cebap Classification $\geq C_3$).
*   **[ ] Microcirculatory Flow Deficit:** Baseline capillary clearance velocity or ankle-brachial index indicates tissue stagnation, requiring targeted up-regulation of endothelial Nitric Oxide Synthase (eNOS).
*   **[ ] Exhaustion of Standard Therapeutics:** Subject exhibits documented resistance, poor tolerance, or absolute clinical contraindication to standard synthetic vasoactive or compression therapies.
*   **[ ] Baseline Organ Performance Verification:** Pre-dosing biochemical panel confirms standard hepatic and renal clearances are functional to process the biphasic polyphenolic matrix.

## 2. ABSOLUTE EXCLUSION CRITERIA (IMMEDIATE REJECTION)
Presence of any single parameter instantly revokes protocol authorization:
*   **[ ] Active Bleeding Diatheses:** Due to the competitive eNOS-upregulation mechanism and down-regulation of platelet aggregation cascades, subjects with active internal ulcerations or bleeding disorders are excluded.
*   **[ ] Scheduled Major Surgical Procedures:** Protocol must be suspended $\geq 14\text{ days}$ prior to any scheduled major surgical intervention to allow cGMP and PGI₂ vascular baselines to normalize.
*   **[ ] Pediatric Cohort:** Complete structural exclusion for individuals under 18 years of age due to unmapped clearance parameters of the microbially cleaved $\gamma$-valerolactone metabolite peak.
